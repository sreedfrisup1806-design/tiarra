Our Story

A Legacy Born from
Passion & Heritage

Tiarra was born from a simple belief: that every woman deserves to feel extraordinary on the most important days of her life. Founded in Nagercoil, at the heart of Tamil Nadu's rich textile and jewellery tradition, we set out to be more than a store — we wanted to be a part of your family's story.

From the grand Kanjivaram silks woven along the banks of the Kaveri to the intricate temple gold jewellery of the deep south, our collections are a love letter to Indian craftsmanship. Every piece we curate is chosen with meticulous care, honouring the artisans who pour generations of skill into their work.

Over the years, Tiarra has grown into a destination that brides and families trust not just for the quality of our pieces, but for the warmth of our experience — where choosing your wedding jewellery feels like a celebration in itself.

Our Guiding Principles

Uncompromised Quality

Every piece — from a delicate stud to a grand bridal set — is sourced from certified artisans and verified for purity, ensuring it becomes a treasured heirloom.

We work directly with master weavers and goldsmiths from Tamil Nadu's most celebrated traditions, preserving techniques passed down through centuries.

A Personal Experience

Shopping at Tiarra is never transactional. Our stylists take time to understand your story, your wedding, your family — and curate selections that feel entirely yours.

The Craft

Handcrafted by
Master Artisans

Behind every Tiarra piece is a craftsperson whose family has woven silk or shaped gold for generations. We travel to the workshops of Kanchipuram, Dharmavaram, and the goldsmith communities of Nagercoil to source directly — ensuring authenticity and fair artisan partnerships.

Our jewellery is hallmarked and our sarees carry GI certification where applicable. When you wear Tiarra, you carry not just beauty, but the living tradition of Indian craftsmanship.
