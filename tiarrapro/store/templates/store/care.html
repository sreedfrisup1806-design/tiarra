Jewellery Care Guide

Preserve the beauty and shine of your precious pieces

Why Jewellery Care Matters

Your jewellery is more than an accessory — it's a memory, a tradition, and a statement. Proper care ensures longevity, shine, and lasting elegance for years to come.

Essential Care Tips

Proper Storage

Store jewellery in soft-lined boxes or pouches to avoid scratches.

Gentle Cleaning

Use a soft cloth and mild soap solution for regular cleaning.

Avoid Chemicals

Keep jewellery away from perfumes, sprays, and harsh chemicals.

Careful Handling

Remove jewellery before workouts, swimming, or heavy tasks.

Care for Different Jewellery Types

  • Gold: Clean gently and store separately.
  • Silver: Keep in anti-tarnish cloth or boxes.
  • Diamonds: Avoid touching frequently to prevent oil buildup.
  • Silk Sarees: Store in muslin cloth and avoid moisture.
